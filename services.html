Estate Management

We offer a vast amount of services under Real Estate & Estate Management; We provide quality services by rendering professional services, as such;

Maintenance:

The provision of high quality and reliable contractors to undertake all regular ongoing maintenance work for any estate is one of the most important items for the efficient running of the buildings and apartments. We include regular visits to estates to ensure any items of repair are noted and dealt with quickly and efficiently, and liaising with professional advisors and suitably qualified contractors to ensure the highest standards are maintained.
These maintenance items include:
Gardening and grounds maintenance (as required). Cleaning of interior communal hallways, stairwells and windows (as required). Interior and exterior decorating.
Minor insurance works.
General building and maintenance.

General:

We will arrange Buildings and Public Liability insurance, Directors’ and Officers’ Liability insurance, Lift Inspection insurance (if applicable), through reputable brokers and obtain quotations prior to renewal to ensure that the best possible cover is provided.
We will provide a liaison point for residents for all enquiries relating to the management of the estate.

Services requiring additional fees:

Items requiring additional work on our part, e.g. major works, decorating, significant insurance claims, planning applications, etc, would normally be subject to separate additional fees, calculated on a percentage of the total cost of the works and subject to mutual agreement before commencement of the work, wherever practicable. Any works requiring overseeing by a suitably qualified professional would also be subject to separate additional fees calculated on an agreed percentage of the total cost of the works. The following services are subject to a separate charge, normally payable by the individual resident.

  • Dealing with purchasers’ solicitors enquiries.
  • Dealing with all matters following the sale of individual properties, including the transfer and issue of new share/membership certificates, as required.
